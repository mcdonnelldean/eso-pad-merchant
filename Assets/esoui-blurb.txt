Get MasterMerchant data while in Gamepad Mode! Simply install MasterMerchant and PadMerchant while using Gamepad Mode. Adds historical data to tooltips and improves Browse Results and Listing panels of the Guild Store.

[SIZE="4"]Features[/SIZE]
[LIST]
[*] Stops MasterMerchant from erroring on load when in gamepad mode
[*] Adds per unit price to Trade Store Browse Results & Own Listings panels
[*] Adds support for MasterMerchant data in the following on Tooltips, Browse Results & Own Listings
[/LIST]
